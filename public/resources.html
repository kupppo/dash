Custom Sprites
SpriteSomething is a utility which allows custom sprites to be used in retro games like Super Metroid. All DASH seeds are compatible with SpriteSomething.

NOTE: Make sure to apply your custom sprites to generated DASH roms and not the vanilla rom!
